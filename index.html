Multi-dimensional scores for your products

To infer products sustainability we analyze and assess the sustainability labels of a product like the GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), Blue Angel and more. Our scientific approach evaluates the credibility and social and ecological aspects of a label according to a question catalog of 300+ questions. Our scores range from 0 (bad) to 100 (good).

Seamless integration via API

Marketplaces and Retailers can integrate our credible evaluations for sustainability labels seamlessly via our API (Application Programming Interface). They send a request with a sustainability label to our API and retrieve a response that includes our evaluation scores.

Ensuring transparency and credibility in sustainability claims

As a part of the 'Green Consumption Assistant', a BMUV AI lighthouse project, we collaborated with Ecosia to integrate sustainable product data into their search results. Sustainable offerings were presented in the top 5 search results. Our experiment demonstrated a significant increase in click-through rates (CTR) on the sustainable products.

Sustainability remains a challenge for e-commerce

Despite a third of online shoppers are being influenced by sustainability to make a purchase decision, 53% of green product claims in the EU are considered ambiguous. With over 200+ certifications, businesses struggle to provide credible product data to enable their customers for an informed decision.
